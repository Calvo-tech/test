Property Management

The sole objective of Rivertech Consulting Ltd is to provide property owners and tenants' unique level of service responsiveness and accountability based on simple promise that a true satisfied customer is the one whose property is well maintained and is always in good tenable condition and brings back a sound financial return with happy tenants.

Our system of management is based on best people equipped with proper information and resources through being up to date on required technology and market trends.

Roles in which Rivertech will play ;

  • Marketing
  • Tenant Occupancy
  • Facility
  • Administration & Risk management

KEY FOCUS

  1. Tom make sure the you(the property owner) minimizes expenses and maximizes profits
  2. To make sure that tenants pay as per agreed time as well as implementing policy that will secure all rental income this is enhance 100% collection
  3. Leasing your property to the best suitable tenant as quickly as possible after screening them to avoid leasing out to dangerous occupants
  4. Implementing & enforcing owner's instructions promptly.

Our Management Services And Procedures

1. Advertising

We will advertise your property in our websites for free and aslo at any other Marketing platform i.e posters, billboards, news paper and last but not list social media platform i.e facebook. Photos of the property are taken and kept in order for future reference also marketing purposes.

2. Tenant selection & screening

Strict tenant selection process is in place whereby a Tenancy Agreement is issued to the prospective tenant and tenant is rrequired to give out all the necessary information alongside the original Id and sign.

3. Rent Payment

Tenants must pay their rent on time through several methods into a defined collection account as will be agreed. These include but are not limited to bank Payment, RTGS, direct banking,cash or cheque payment at our office. Whichever method adopted will conform strictly to the aforementioned area of responsibility that is risk management and will not give room for any kind of fraud the managers are committed to ensure this. Rent arrears are followed up and appropriate action is taken to make sure that rent is prompt.

4. Letting Fee

The letting fee applies to property owners who require their property to be attained new tenants only and management is done by himself. For hte property ownwer who reqires new tenants and the same tenants to be managed by our firm, letting fee does not apply.

5. Management Fees

Our management fees are very friendly and covers day to day running of the property that is liasing with tenants,site visits, processing and collecting rent payment & arrears. Management fee is deducted on all the rental monies collected on behalf this is however not mandatory and negotiations will be done on case by case basis. Rent deposits, water or electricity monies collected on your behalf do not qualify for management fee.

6. Maintenance And Repairs

It's your choice on who to do the repairs but if you wish that our firm undertake the repairs and maintenance on your behalf an extra fee may apply.

7. Electricity and water bill

It's the responsibility of the tenant to pay electricity & water bill and the property owner only pays for the security lights, power used by water pump and sewer charges, but case whereby the responsibility lies on the property owner, we can pay on his/her behalf at request and recover the same from the respective account with no extra cost.

8. Monthly Statements

Payment are made at an agreed date; statements are forwarded to the property owner showing amounts collected net all the deductions such as Management fees, any payments made by us on his/her behalf through advances or transfer to the bank etc.
